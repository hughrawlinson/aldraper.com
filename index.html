Al Draper Actor Extroardinaire


Al grew up in the maritime town of Schull in West Cork. In his youth, he was extremely active in the arts, and participated primarily in drama and music. He is an accomplished player of both the mandolin and the banjo, as well as singing in numerous bands and musical stage productions over the course of his high-school education. Al participated in every theatrical production that was available to him, experiencing a wide variety of genres and a diverse range of characters. He also participated as an actor in numerous short films produced by locals for the Corona Short Film Festival which takes place annually in Schull.
